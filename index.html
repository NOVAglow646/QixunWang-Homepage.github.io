About me

I'm an second-year Ph.D. student in the School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Peking University.

Research interests

  • Mechanism and applications of large language models (LLMs) and multimodal LLMs.

    Including in-context learning (ICL), hallucination, complex reasoning and test-time scaling techniques.

  • Theory and algorithmic design of out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization.

    Including generalization problems across various machine learning models and scenarios, like domain generalization (DG) in computer vision tasks, graph OOD generalization, and generalization of LLMs.
